About Napitalai Engineers

R. Napitalai Limited was established in 1998 Under the IPA company Registration Act as an engineering consulting firm primarily specialising in the areas of Structural and Civil Engineering, construction and Project Management consultants. Since its inception in 1988, the firm has undertaken consultancy work for clients both in Public and Private Sector for the last 16 years completing all tasks within time and budgetary restraints. Since 1988, R. Napitalai Limited has had an extensive career, working in partnership for development with both the Public Sector (PNG Government) and Industry (Private Sector) as designed engineers, project managers and QA engineers or supervision engineers. The scope of works and undertakings was extensive, it covers working in both the urban and even remote locations throughout his environmentally challenging nation of Papua New Guinea. Some such projects covered telecommunication towers on remote mountaintops, slope stability checks in deep mining open cut pits, swing and fixed bridges across fast flowing rivers, high rise building in restricted and confined urban spaces and even in deep foundations for multi-storey buildings over reclaimed land over the sea.

Professional Experience

01

RABAUL VOLCANOLOGICAL OBSEVATORY, KOKOPO, ENB (EST VALUE K50m +)

Lead Structural engineer on remedial works design on an 87m long RC retaining wall of height range of 4.6m 5.4m. Initial work after detail investigation involved remedial works design, testing and actual reconstruction of the wall. Work onsite is currently completed on the affected wall. Current works involves the design and construction of other erosion protection structures including, RC retaining walls, Gabion walls and stone pitched gravity walls. Design works has commences on the RVO monitoring building as the lead structural engineer.

02

TELIKOM PNG COUNTRY ROLL OUT (Commercial Tower Project Est Value K100m +)

Leading Structural engineers on the design and maintenance of TELIKOM PNG Country roll out program to upgrade mobile communication nationwide . The works involved foundation designs, lattice structure, Quality Assurance (QA) checks, foundation upgrade and strengthening works, slope stability and protection works throughput PNG.

03

KUMUL DATACO HUAWEI UNDERSEA FIBRE OPTIC CABLING WORKS (Estimated Value:K200m +)

We were tasked to design and supervise the Cable Landing Stations (CLS) once the undersea cables came aground. This included bridging structures, pits and manholes and the CLS building to house the monitoring equipment and facilities. In addition to CLS facilities Dataco embarked on redundancy routes to the support fiber optic system. We were also responsible for the satellite platforms and VSAT platforms at Kokopo, Kavieng, Mt, Hagen, Wabag and Mendi.

04

WHARVES AND JETTIES PROJECT (Numerous Clients Estimated Value: Unknown

Numerous wharfs and jetty projects have been undertaken for PNG Ports, NMSA, and other clients. I was involved either as the design engineer , QA check engineer or certify engineer on these projects . Some include Kupiano Wharf rehabilitation, Omorong jetty (Kokopo), Kavieng wharf, Finchafen wharf and Kikori jetty.

05

MULTI STOREY BUOLDINGS (Numerous Clients Estimated Value: K100m +)

OTML Tabubil conducted a technical audit on their conveyor support structure, ball mill structures and tailing structure. Usage over 20 years subjected the structural systems to corrosions. Work involved checking and ensuring the remedial options, strengthening and safety methods of employed do not hinder mining process.

06

MULTI STOREY BUOLDINGS (Numerous Clients Estimated Value: K100m +)

MCGL Hidden Valley. We were required to do an audit trestles and benches from the pit to the processing facility at Hamata. A detailed audit was done and their asset register updated. RNL was the lead engineer designing the structural system for the building in association with Terrence Karo architects.

RNL Services

At R. Napitalai Limited we are dedicated to providing professional and innovative service for our clients. We cover a wide variety of Civil Residential, Commercial Industrial, Geotechnical and Environmentalprojects. Our service include, but not limited to, Site Investigations, Conceptual Design, Project Management, Construction and Documentations and liaison with Architects, Builders, Contractors and government authorities. As our extensive engineering experience allows us to give you the solution to fit your Commercial, Industrial and Residential needs, we also work a large number of Architectural Design Projects, Renovation alteration and additions, shops, office, site plans, Storm water analysis, Storm water drainage and collection system, Storm Water Detention and Retention Basin, Erosion and Sediments Control plans, Minor Road Designs, Retaining Walls, Pedestrian Bridges, Driveway and Carpark Designs tilt up construction and more. We have done many projects where we not only provide the civil and structural engineering services but also responsible for the management of the project from the concept to completion. Such projects requires us to be Project Engineers or Supervisors where we do on-site supervision of works, measurements of quantities, ensuring quality control and testing becomes paramount.

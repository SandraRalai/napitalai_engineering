Our Story

R. Napitalai Limited was established in 1998 Under the IPA company Registration Act as an engineering consulting firm primarily specialising in the areas of Structural and Civil Engineering, construction and Project Management consultants. Since its inception in 1988, the firm has undertaken consultancy work for clients both in Public and Private Sector for the last 16 years completing all tasks within time and budgetary restraints. Since 1988, R. Napitalai Limited has had an extensive career, working in partnership for development with both the Public Sector (PNG Government) and Industry (Private Sector) as designed engineers, project managers and QA engineers or supervision engineers. The scope of works and undertakings was extensive, it covers working in both the urban and even remote locations throughout his environmentally challenging nation of Papua New Guinea. Some such projects covered telecommunication towers on remote mountaintops, slope stability checks in deep mining open cut pits, swing and fixed bridges across fast flowing rivers, high rise building in restricted and confined urban spaces and even in deep foundations for multi-storey buildings over reclaimed land over the sea.

About Napitalai Engineers

Engineering for Economy and Elegance

Technical Capability

Our technical capabilities cover Civil and Structural engineering analysis, design and documentation work which includes, to mention a few, roads/highways, water and sewerage, sub-divisional development, bridges/wharves and jetties, Geotechnical Investigation, Project Management and Supervision. Our professional and technical staffs have extensive experience at the different stage of engineering and project development from feasibility study, investigation, design, construction and maintenance (remedial works)

Materials and Design Aids

In order to produce the best quality of service, we have placed emphasis and invested personnel equipment knowledge relevant to our line of work since inception. With the current rapid change in technology, there is a need for us to be updated with the latest design trends and equipment since the days when drawings were done on manual drawing boards

Computer Software Used With Specific Applications

  • Structural Analysis, SAP2000, SpaceGass
  • Civil Designs, Civil CAD used for civil engineering designs
  • Autodesk Civil 3D 2021 and 2017
  • Project Management, MS Projects and Microsoft Projects
  • Accounting ad payroll, quick books Pro for windows
  • Revit and ArchiCAD

Safety and Ethics

All works that was done is in strict accordance with the Constitution and Code of Ethics of the Institution of engineers (IEPNG) in Papua New Guinea. Structural Engineering designs are subject to structural check and certification process to ensure that the design and documentations are comply with the Structural Engineering Provisions of Building Act Chapter 301 of the revised Laws of Papua New Guinea.

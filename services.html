Civil Engineering

Designing Structures Project Planning & Management Supervising the Construction of Projects. Quality Control & Safety Assurance

Structural Engineering

ensure structures can withstand the stresses and pressures imposed by use and the environment.

Project Management

overseeing the planning, design, and construction of various civil engineering projects, including buildings, bridges, roads, and infrastructure

Inspection & Reporting

identify problems with structures like bridges, oil pipelines and roads and share their results with construction teams in an attempt to prevent accidents and other catastrophes.

Construction

Civil Construction is a part of civil engineering that deals with planning, designing, constructing and maintaining physical structures as well as natural components

Drafting & Architectural Designs

Architectural drafters — also called CAD drafters, CAD operators or AutoCAD drafters — create technical drawings that pull together all the needed information about how to build a commercial, residential or industrial structure. They bring accuracy and precise specifications to an idea that started as a sketch.

Safety and Ethics

All works that was done is in strict accordance with the Constitution and Code of Ethics of the Institution of engineers (IEPNG) in Papua New Guinea. Structural Engineering designs are subject to structural check and certification process to ensure that the design and documentations are comply with the Structural Engineering Provisions of Building Act Chapter 301 of the revised Laws of Papua New Guinea. In prior to work being handed to the client, we have our registered supervising engineer or principle engineer to carry out all final and quality assurance checks on work performed by Napitalai engineers.
